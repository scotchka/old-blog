What to order at a Chinese restaurant?

Find a Chinese restaurant menu. Copy and paste a section into the box below, with blank lines separating the items. Click "Rate" to see each dish ranked (from one to three stars) according to how mainstream or novel it is. For example, "Chow Mein" is ubiquitous, whereas "Vegan Pork Belly" would likely be unique to that restaurant. Note that the novelty is calculated relative among the items you submit, so the more items, the more accurate the results will be. We also display an overall rating which compares your submitted menu to other restaurant menus.
